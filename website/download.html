Open Hestia, then add it to your home screen

Add Hestia to your home screen and it opens just like any other app — three taps, on your phone or tablet.

  1. 1

    Tap the orange Open Hestia button above. It opens in your web browser.

  2. 2

    Press the Share button .

  3. 3

    Choose Add to Home Screen. Hestia now sits on your home screen, like any other app.

The Share button is the square with an arrow pointing up — at the bottom of the screen on an iPhone, or in the menu on Android.

Stuck, or not sure?

Installing is optional — you can always just tap Open Hestia and use it in your browser. If you'd like a hand, ask a family member, or read the common questions.
