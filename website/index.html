How it works

Detect. Warn. Shut off.

Three steps run continuously on the device in your kitchen. Watch the loop play out.

  1. 1Detect

    Sensors watch the burner and the room — is the stove lit, and is anyone near it?

  2. 2Warn

    If a lit stove is left unattended, the buzzer sounds and an absence countdown begins.

  3. 3Shut off

    If no one returns before the countdown ends, Hestia cuts power to the stove.

Built for the whole household

Everything you need to trust the stove.

One clear status, shared with the people who matter — and the controls to act on it.

  • Live status at a glance

    The current state of every stove — readable in half a second, from anywhere.

  • Tap to pair

    Tap your phone to the device to pair it over NFC — no codes to copy.

  • Households & roles

    Admins manage devices and members; everyone else can see status and act. Roles are legible at a glance.

  • Timers & reminders

    Set a cook timer and Hestia keeps watch alongside you — a reminder before the countdown starts.

    Tune how long “unattended” is allowed before the countdown starts. Long, low-heat cooking won’t trigger a countdown.

  • Event history

    Every warning, return, and auto-shutoff, logged with timestamps — so you always know what happened.

Local-first by design

The decision that matters never waits on Wi-Fi.

The safety loop — detect, warn, shut off — runs entirely on the device in your kitchen. The app is the window onto it, not the brain behind it.

  • Works if the internet drops. The stove still shuts off. Connectivity changes what you can see, never whether you’re safe.

  • No kitchen cameras. Presence is sensed locally; nothing streams your kitchen to a server.

  • Your home, your data. Status and history stay with your household.
